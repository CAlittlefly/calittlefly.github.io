How to enter into Financial Analyst job

Question: They say that as the corporate financial analyst, highly be valued the digital analysis ability, among so many resumes, how do you sniff out the right candidates for a follow-up interview?

We will pay attention to whether the internship experiences specified in the resume are relevant to work; including some basic Excel skills, the degree of understanding of the financial systems; such as SAP, Oracle, Salesforce or the ability to use SQL, of course, this depends on the specific requirements of the work. We also pay special attention to whether or not the candidate looks energetic.

Question:You recruited many new graduates in the past years. From your point of view, what are the best 'little potatoes' from interviews? What are the characteristics of your special attention?

'Look for new opportunities to develop him / herself always' . which means they are willing to make further improvements in the company. HP is a global company, covering a lot of business, so especially as a new worker, do not worry about whether there is a bottleneck. HP has a internal policy to encourage and provide educations for career growth, so every 12-18 months, each financial analyst needs to take the initiative for in-house cross-departmental, cross-business training to find new opportunities to improve themselves. The workplace is a new battlefield, how to find their own suitable career platform, acceleration of career development; interest, skills, knowledge, and leadership.

How to step into Investment Banking career path?

Question: Top-ranking school, a strong digital capacity, CFA, MSF; is the basic standard of a typical investment banker. Do you know of any 'special talented' individuals employed there?

The Investment Banking Department (IBD) is generally staffed from undergraduate students in the career field, starting from the Analyst Program. It is usually 2 years to be promoted to Associate (this level is commonly known as the Senior Analyst), of course, this is also where a lot of famous MBA graduates want to engage in financial work of their choice. This process, from the beginning to the final result, requires at least 1 and a half months of interviews, with experience and Ministry of Personnel, project leader, departmental executives, etc.; the acceptance rate is relatively low. After entering the Analyst Program, the company will consider the strength of the employee’s adaptability, the choice of the workplace, the degree of interest within the investment bank and the performance of the work, and so on. If after getting through the first few years, earning outstanding performance, then congratulations to you entering into the advanced path.

Question:Investment banking is divided into investment management, transaction operations, risk management, and several other core functional departments. I would like to ask them, what are their respective fields’ new employee focus requirements? For example, where are your function amenities, is it usually young people’s career or non-professional career ability?

Investment banks are not full of all-famous students. The United States has some good graduated employees from out of the smart people, many entered the operations-related business units acting as traders (commonly known as salespeople), as well as technical personnel to support the transaction business. Traders on Wall Street are a unique group of people, they have a variety of professional backgrounds, because the sales work values performance. So there is high value on the ability to deal with people.
